## Match Types

- Time trial: two laps on the outer lane, counter-clockwise, within 4 minutes
- Mission driving: obstacle avoidance, traffic light, vertical parking
- Each match type is run separately, and scores are combined

## Important Rules

- Vehicle must drive autonomously with mounted software control only.
- Hardware configuration after inspection must be maintained for all matches.
- SMPS voltage is fixed at 12.0 V. Boosting is not allowed.
- Vehicle must be ready within 2 minutes after placement at the start line.
- If the vehicle does not start within 10 seconds after the start signal, one retry may be given.
- Lane departure may allow repositioning up to 3 times, depending on match context.

## Mission-Specific Notes

- Obstacle mission ends when the vehicle passes the M3 lap timer.
- Obstacle positions for vehicles 2 and 3 are selected on match day.
- Traffic-light mission requires stopping before the crosswalk, then starting within 5 seconds after green.
- Vertical parking must be done by steering. Differential spin parking is not allowed.
- Parking start position is selected on match day, and program modification after position selection is not allowed.
